Instructions

  • Applicants will have to pursue 3 steps for completing the Registration Process:
    • Step 1: XML generation in Offline Utility
    • Step 2: Tasks to be completed at haryanatax.gov.in
    • Step 3: Tasks to be performed at FCO
  • Step 1: XML generation in Offline Utility
    1. Applicants are required to fill details in the main form and the related annexures.
    2. Every form has a "Validate" button at the bottom. Users must validate each form to confirm all mandatory fields are completed.
    3. Wherever multiple items are to be saved, there is a provision of a table.
      • To populate details in the table, enter values in fields below the table and click on "Save".
    4. "View/Edit" can be clicked to view and edit saved information.
    5. "View/Edit" buttons are available next to each Name in Annexure I & V tables. Use these to view and fill respective annexures per applicant/person.
    6. To remove a name from Annexure I/V, delete the corresponding entry in the table at serial number 22/23 of Form VAT AT.
    7. The form can be filled with or without Digital Signatures.
    8. Digital Signatures can be used partially (for some applicants only).
    9. Once all annexures and forms are filled, click "Validate and Generate XML" to generate the final XML.
  • Step 2: Tasks to be completed at haryanatax.gov.in
    1. Applicants are required to:
      1. Create a Temporary User Account
      2. Upload the XML
      3. Pay Registration Fees under HVAT Act, 2003 & CST Act, 1956 at https://egrashry.nic.in
        • STEP 1:
          The applicants will generate challan from the above site
          Printout of such challan will be carried by the applicant to the concerned bank/treasury
          Payment for Registration fee will be accepted the bank before expiry of the challan
          The bank will issue a CIN and CIN date for payment receipt
        • STEP 2:
          The applicants will re login into the temporary account
          The applicants will then update GRN, GRN Date, CIN, CIN date, Bank name
      4. (Optional) Upload Documents
    1. Once all tasks are completed, the applicant will be able to preview the Application Form.
    2. Printouts of application forms will be available post submission at www.haryanatax.gov.in
    3. The applicants will be required to submit signed copy of the printout of application forms and mandatory documents within 7 days to the Excise & Taxation Department, Haryana through the Facilitation Centre Operator (FCO) of the concerned district.
      Please note, in case the printout of the Application forms and/or signed copies of Mandatory documents are not submitted to the FCO within 7 days, the form will be automatically scrapped.
      In such scenario, all information submitted by the applicant will become void including Registration fees paid. The applicant will have to perform all actions again including fresh payment of Registration Fees.
    4. In case the XML uploaded and the documents uploaded are endorsed by Digital Signature, then the form will be submitted through temporary login and Application No.
      Scanned copy of duly signed printout of CST form A is mandatory to be uploaded in this cate which should be endorsed by Digital Signature
  • STEP 3: Tasks to be performed at FCO
    1. Duly Signed Printouts of Application Forms (including VAT A1, its Annexures & CST forms A) are required to be submitted to the FCO.
    2. All persons mentioned in table appearing at serial number 11 of CST Form A are also required to provide their signatures (duty attested by witnesses) on the printout of CST Form A.
    3. Applicants/Authorized persons who have not attached digital signatures in the XML are required to provide their signatures on the printout of the Application forms including Annexures.
    4. Applicants/Authorized Persons who have not attached digital singatures in the XML are required to provide their singatures on the printout of the
  • Documents
    1. The applicants will be required to submit signed copies of doomments to the FCO along with Application Forms.
    2. The FCO will upload documents which have not been uploaded by the applicant.
  • Acknowledgement

    2. The Application for Registration will the considered to be submitted to Excise & Taxation Department, Haryana on the date and time the acknowledgement numbers
